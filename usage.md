Quick examples:

```text
@help
@get diamond_shovel
@toolset iron
@stacked
@shulker store diamond 3
@shulker retrieve stick 8
@shulker auto on
@player
@stop
```

Press `C` for the in-game UI and `+` to globally abort a running task.
